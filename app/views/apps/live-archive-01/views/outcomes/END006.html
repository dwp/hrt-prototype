Evidence to collect

  • passport – number, nationality, date of issue and expiry date
  • bank account statement or simple cash book covering at least 3 months

If they haven't brought all the evidence

  • Book further evidence interview and in the 'description' field, enter 'Create prove you can apply to-do'.
  • Tell them to bring all the evidence to the interview
  • Click ‘done’ to complete the ‘prove you can apply’ to-do.

If they've brought all the evidence (fast-track)

  1. Go to the ‘prove you can apply’ to-do and in the ‘agent notes’ box, enter:
    • the claimant status (see the top of this page for claimant status)
    • the details of evidence you’ve collected
  2. Click ‘done’ to complete the ‘prove you can apply’ to-do.
  3. Email the decision maker at stockport.pyca@dwp.gsi.gov.uk with the full name of the person claiming.
  4. Book a habitual residency test interview in the UCFS build (not Booking Bug) using any date and time.
  5. Go to the claimant’s to-do list, open ‘Attend your habitual residency interview’ to-do, select ‘attended’ and save.
  6. Remind the person claiming to check their Universal Credit account for updates on their claim.
