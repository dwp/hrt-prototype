PYCA-1246

Changes

As a UK national that has forgotten their passport, give them an option for a further evidence appointment.

Hypothesis

We have seen that British Nationals are being sent for a full HRT interview. We ask UK nationals if they have a UK passport, and if they don’t have it with them, we send them for a full HRT interview. However, when they have later provided evidence it enables them to be fast tracked.
So if we ask them to do bring in their evidence (to a FEI?)
We’ll see a reduction of UK nationals incorrectly being sent to for a full HRT interview and instead being fast tracked or a decision could be made by the WC
