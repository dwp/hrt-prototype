Multiple “out of UK” questions are confusing work coaches
Related stories
PYCA-1002
PYCA-1061
PYCA-1074

Changes

Change the time spent out of the UK questions from yes/no to multiple choice.

Also includes alternative variants for “You can now record the decision” from PYCA-1074 and new statistics gathering question from PYCA-1002.

Hypothesis

We think work coaches read the “2 years out of the UK” question the same as the “4 weeks in the last 2 years” question, picking the wrong answer.

Research notes

Three work coaches in Rusholme all read the new question wrong. One work coach corrected themselves after a pause.
