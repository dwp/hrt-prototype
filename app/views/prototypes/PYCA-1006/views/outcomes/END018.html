Complete the ‘Refer to Decision Maker (HRT)’ to-do

  1. Download the ALP.
  2. Complete the appropriate section in the ALP using the necessary evidence.
  3. Upload the ALP.
  4. Enter the claimant’s nationality.
  5. For the question ‘Is the claimant a refugee?’, select ‘Yes’.
  6. For the question ‘Have you seen a Biometric Residence Permit (BRP) for the claimant’, select ‘Yes’.
  7. Input the expiry date of the Biometric Residence Permit (BRP).
  8. Select ‘Done’.
