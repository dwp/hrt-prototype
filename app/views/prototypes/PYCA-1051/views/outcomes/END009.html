Claimant status

Limited leave to enter/remain

Evidence to record in the ALP

All of the following:

  • passport – number, nationality, issue date, expiry date and visa status (if recorded)
  • biometric residence permit (BRP) – permit number (right hand corner), nationality, place of issue, issue date, expiry date, permit type, remarks on the front and back of the card
  • date they most recently left the UK and their destination, date they returned, reason they left and returned
  • any previous periods of absence
