Go to the ‘check if claimant needs HRT interview’ to-do

  1. For the question “Does the ‘prove you can apply’ service say ‘this person has proved they can apply for Universal Credit’?”, select ‘No’.
  2. Select the category ‘This person does not need an HRT’.
  3. Select ‘Done’.

Create a claimant to-do

  1. Select template ‘Update information about your nationality’.
  2. Copy the following into the ‘Why does the claimant need to update their nationality information?’ box:

    ‘Claimant nationality is British Citizen and has not been out of the country for more than 4 weeks in the last 2 years’.

  3. Set the deadline date to the end of the first 2 assessment periods.
  4. Select ‘create to-do’.

Tell the claimant to sign in to their online account and complete this to-do

  1. For the question ‘What’s your nationality?’, tell them to select ‘British’.
  2. For the question ‘Have you been out of the UK in the last 2 years?’, tell them to select ‘No’.
  3. Remind the claimant to check their online account for updates on their claim.
