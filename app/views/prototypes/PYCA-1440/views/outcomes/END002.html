Claimant status

EEA worker, Redundant or Temporarily sick EEA citizen

Go to the ‘check if HRT interview is needed’ to-do

  1. For the question “What is the outcome from the ‘prove you can apply’ service?”, select ‘Fast-track this person’.
  2. Select ‘Done’.

Complete the ‘refer to decision maker (HRT)’ to-do

  1. Download the ALP.
  2. Complete the appropriate section in the ALP using the necessary evidence.
  3. Upload the ALP.
  4. Enter the claimant’s nationality.
  5. Select ‘Done’.

Evidence to record in the ALP

One of the following:

  • passport
  • national ID card

Include number, nationality, issue date and expiry date.

Plus one from the following list:

  • payslips covering the last 3 months - name of employer and earnings (including pay frequency, date and amount)
  • bank statements showing earnings covering the last 3 months (including pay dates and amounts)
  • a permanent contract or letter from their employer (confirming type and duration of contract, start date, hours of work and earnings), as well as payslips or bank statements covering the last month
