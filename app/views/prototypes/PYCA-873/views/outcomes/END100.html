Evidence to record in the ALP

  • passport – number, nationality, date of issue and expiry date
  • BRP or residence card — issue date, unique permit number (right hand corner), type of permit, remarks, comments on the back
  • exact date they came to live in the UK (otherwise month and year, or just year)

Clear the Prove You Can Apply to-do

  1. For the question ‘Is an HRT interview required?’, select ‘No’.
  2. Click ‘Done’.

Clear the Refer to Decision Maker to-do

  1. Download the ALP.
  2. Complete the appropriate section in the ALP using the necessary evidence.
  3. Upload the ALP.
  4. Enter the claimant’s nationality.
  5. For the question ‘Is the claimant a refugee?’, select ‘No’.
  6. For the question ‘Have you seen a Biometric Residence Permit (BRP) for the claimant’, select ‘Yes’.
  7. Input the expiry date of the Biometric Residence Permit (BRP).
  8. Click ‘Done’.
