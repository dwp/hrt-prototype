Claimant status

Refugee

Evidence to collect

  • passport or travel document (if they have any) – number, nationality, date of issue, expiry date and visa status or
  • residence permit card – issue date, unique permit number on the right corner of the card, type of permit and any details at the back of the card or
  • Home Office document that says they’re a refugee, including dates

Clear the Prove You Can Apply to-do

  1. For the question ‘Is a HRT interview required?’, select ‘No’.
  2. Click ‘Done’.

Clear the Refer to Decision Maker to-do

  1. Download the ALP.
  2. Complete the appropriate section in the ALP using the necessary evidence.
  3. Upload the ALP.
  4. For the question ‘Is the claimant a refugee?’, select ‘Yes’.
  5. For the question ‘Have you seen a Biometric Residence Permit (BRP) for the claimant’, select ‘No’.
  6. For the question ‘Is this a fast-track HRT referral case?’, select ‘Yes’.
  7. Click ‘Done’.
