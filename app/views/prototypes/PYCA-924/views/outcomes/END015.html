Clear the Prove You Can Apply to-do

  1. For the question ‘Is a HRT interview required?’, select ‘No’.
  2. Click ‘Done’.

Create a claimant to-do:

  1. Select template ‘Update information about your nationality’.
  2. Copy the following into the ‘Why does the claimant need to update their nationality information?’ box:

    ‘Claimant nationality is British Citizen and has not been out of the country for more than 4 weeks in the last 2 years’.

  3. Set the deadline date to the end of the first two assessment periods.
  4. Select ‘create to-do’.

Advise the person claiming, to sign into their account and clear this to-do:

  1. For the question ‘Are you a British Citizen?’, tell them to select ‘Yes’.
  2. For the question ‘In the last 2 years, have you been out of the UK for more than 4 weeks at a time?’, tell them to select ‘No’.
  3. Remind the person claiming, to check their UC account for updates on their claim.
