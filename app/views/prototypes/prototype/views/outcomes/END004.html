Claimant status

No recourse to public funds

Evidence to record in the ALP

One of the following:

  • visa page within their passport – number, nationality, place of issue, issue date, expiry date and permit or visa type
  • biometric residence permit (BRP) – permit number (right hand corner), nationality, place of issue, issue date, expiry date, permit type, remarks on the front and back of the card
  • Home Office letter confirming their immigration status, including dates

Go to the ‘check if HRT interview is needed’ to-do

  1. For the question “Does the ‘prove you can apply’ service say ‘this person has proved they can apply for Universal Credit’?”, select ‘No’.
  2. Select the category ‘Fast-track this person’.
  3. Select ‘Done’.

Complete the ‘refer to decision maker (HRT)’ to-do

  1. Download the ALP.
  2. Complete the appropriate section in the ALP using the necessary evidence.
  3. Upload the ALP.
  4. Enter the claimant’s nationality.
  5. Select ‘Done’.
